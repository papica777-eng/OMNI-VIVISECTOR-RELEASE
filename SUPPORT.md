# Enterprise Support & Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Welcome to AETERNA Enterprise Support. Our priority is to ensure your integration of the OMNI-VIVISECTOR engine remains stable, secure, and performant across your entire DevSecOps pipeline.

## Support Tiers

### 1. `VORTEX_EMPIRE` (Enterprise Tier)
For organizations with active Stripe Subscriptions or Custom Invoicing:
* **Dedicated Slack/Discord Channel:** Direct access to AETERNA Core Engineers.
* **SLA Response Time:** < 4 Hours (24/7/365).
* **Emergency Hotfixes:** Custom over-the-air (OTA) patching for mission-critical engine halts or false positives.
* **CISO Onboarding:** 2 hours of dedicated architecture review and pipeline setup per month.

### 2. `VORTEX_GITHUB_SPONSOR` (Sponsor Tier)
For GitHub Sponsors:
* **Community Discord Access:** Priority access to the Sponsors-only `#omni-support` channel.
* **SLA Response Time:** < 24 Hours (Business Days).
* **Feature Requests:** High-priority consideration on the public roadmap.

## Contact Methods
- **Technical Support:** Open a ticket via your dedicated Dashboard or email `support@aeterna.website`.
- **Sales & Upgrades:** `enterprise@aeterna.website`.
- **Security Incidents:** See `SECURITY.md`.

*When submitting a ticket, please provide your `sk_vortex_...` key prefix (first 12 characters ONLY) to accelerate triage.*
